Frequently asked questions

Is this a subscription or a one-time payment? ›
All BL Creative plans listed here are one-time payments. Once you purchase a license, you can use the software forever with no recurring fees.
Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro or Master later? ›
Yes. You can upgrade at any time by paying only the difference between your current plan and the new one.
Does BL Creative work offline? ›
Yes. Once installed and activated, BL Creative processes your videos locally on your machine—no cloud upload required.
What are the recommended system requirements? ›
We recommend a modern multi-core CPU, at least 16GB RAM, and a dedicated GPU for best performance. The app is optimized for real-world editing workflows.
